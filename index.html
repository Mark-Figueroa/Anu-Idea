Services

All services follow a free consultation, and require an upfront deposit or payment $50-$2500. The following is a list of services offered by Anu Idea:

  1. Web Design and Creation
    • Basic: 1-3 page, website with little to no animated features or customizations. 1-8 weeks in duration. Starts at $500.
    • Complex: 1-10 page, website with a fairly decent amount of animated features and or customization. 1-3 months in duration. Starts at $1500.
    • Comprehensive: 1-10 page, website with integrations and heavy customization. Varies by purpose, maintenance and complexity.
  2. Design Consultation
    • Idea Mapping and Action Plan: We will walkthrough, organize, and fleshout thoughts and ideas revolving around your site, content, business and creative content. Then, we will discuss the tools and resources you need to bring your own idea to life. This is a paid $60/hr meeting.
  3. Content and Creative Work
    • Brand Content
      • Brand Identity: Vision and Mission Statements
      • Logo Design: WE will design a logo that reflects your brand and accurately represents your values
      • Site and Social Banners: Banners for your website pages, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram and Twitter
      • Site Icons and Images: Unlicensed images to be used around your site or on collateral.
    • Marketing Content
      • Email Marketing Copy and Images
      • Email Marketing Setup, Segmentation and Integration
        • Tracking is not included.
    • General Content
      • Content Consultation and Strategy: We will dive into what you hope to achieve and how you can accomplish your goal.
      • General Images and Copy: Images and short taglines, paragraphs to be used as you see fit. Images range from icons and unlicensed photos to digital or hand-drawn illustrations.
    • On-brand ghostwriting
      • This is a specialized service that includes a wide-range of copy creation, drafting, proof-reading and finalization. This process is unbiased and does not include political, moral or ethical judgement on said content, nor does it include research for accuracy. You have an idea, it gets written. Period.
